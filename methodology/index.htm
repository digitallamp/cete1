Methodology

At CETE, we look to solve the problems of now, today, with a dynamic and futures thinking solution that’s adaptable to how the problem could evolve.

Our AI project methodology seeks to prevent the following common errors in project development: a) Finding out several months after implementation that the user does not need several of the features of the system, and that the requirements were not adequately defined b) A significant overshoot of the required system scalability.

A lot of artificial intelligence teams face these kinds of challenges. AI changes nothing about the need to build great products that are fit for the market. But AI project methodology has its subtleties and complexities that make it challenging to apply conventional project techniques and concepts. Nonetheless, the AI project methodology and framework that we have established can be effectively used for all AI projects, and we have developed a way to do this effectively. Read on to grasp the motivation behind our framework and an overview of how we execute our AI project methodology excellently.
